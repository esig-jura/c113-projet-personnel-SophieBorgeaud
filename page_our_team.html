Meet Our Team

At Dunder Mifflin Scranton, our people are our greatest asset. From seasoned sales representatives to meticulous accountants, every team member contributes to the unique culture that makes our branch one of a kind. Meet the professionals who keep the paper flowing, the numbers adding up, and the office atmosphere unforgettable.

Management

Michael Scott – Regional Manager

World’s Best Boss. Leading with unmatched enthusiasm, questionable methods, and an endless supply of motivational… posters.

Sales

Dwight Schrute – Sales Representative and Assistant to the Regional Manager

Dedicated, intense, and a proud beet farmer. Bears, beets, and Battlestar Galactica are his guiding principles in management.

Jim Halpert – Sales Representative

A natural leader with a knack for strategy and office pranks. Expert in keeping morale high and Dwight confused.

Phyllis Vance – Sales Representative

Friendly, reliable, and armed with knitting needles. Phyllis brings warmth and loyalty to every client relationship.

Stanley Hudson – Sales Representative

Calm, professional, and counting the minutes until retirement. Sales meetings are tolerated, Pretzel Day is celebrated.

Andrew Bernard – Sales Representative

Energetic Cornell graduate who sings his way through deals. Sometimes oversells the pitch… literally.

Accounting

Angela Martin – Head of Accounting

Precise, detail-oriented, and a strict enforcer of office rules. Loves cats almost as much as balancing ledgers.

Oscar Martinez – Accountant

The office’s “actually” expert. Logical, meticulous, and always ready to explain the finer points of finance to anyone who will listen.

Kevin Malone – Accountant

Specializes in numbers, M&M’s, and chili recipes. Sometimes his math is… creative, but his heart is in the right place.

Customer Service & Support

Pamela Beesly – Receptionist

Friendly face of the company. Skilled in multitasking, sketching, and avoiding Michael’s unpredictable antics.

Kelly Kapoor – Customer Service Representative

Fashion-forward, talkative, and full of pop culture insights. Customer calls may last longer than expected.

Meredith Palmer – Supplier Relations

Direct, bold, and sometimes too candid. Keeps relations with suppliers… unconventional, but effective.

Human Resources

Toby Flenderson – HR Representative

Calm, professional, and forever ignored by Michael. Dedicated to keeping the office compliant, even if no one listens.

Other Departments

Creed Bratton – Quality Assurance

Mysterious, unpredictable, and possibly involved in activities outside HR guidelines. Brings a unique perspective to quality control.

Ryan Howard – Temporary employment

Ambitious, trendy, and known for “Wuphf.com.” His titles change faster than his loyalty.
